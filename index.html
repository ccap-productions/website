CCAP Productions, LLC is an independent film production company dedicated to crafting original, character-driven stories that resonate with audiences far beyond the screen.

Based in New Jersey, we create films and series that blend genuine humor with authentic human experience — from the dramatic to the delightfully absurd.

Our work has screened at festivals and competitions across the country and the world, earning recognition for excellence in independent filmmaking, writing, and direction.
