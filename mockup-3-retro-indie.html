About

Small Company.
Real Films.

"The best stories don't need a studio — they just need people who care."
— CCAP Productions

CCAP Productions, LLC is an independent film production company that believes the most compelling stories come from honest, specific, character-driven work — not committee-approved formulas.

We make films and series that find the comedy in the human condition: the absurd situations, the characters you recognize from real life, the moments that are equal parts ridiculous and true.

Our work has earned recognition at film festivals and competitions nationwide and around the world, including awards for comedy, writing, and direction.
